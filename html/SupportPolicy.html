Pulse Parenting – Support Policy

Thank you for using Pulse Parenting. Our mission is to support parents in raising emotionally healthy children through expert-backed content and tools. We are committed to providing timely and helpful support.

Contacting Support

If you encounter technical issues, have questions about content, or need assistance using the app, you can reach our support team at:

Email: support@pulseparenting.com

Response Time

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 5 business days. In most cases, responses are much faster. Please include as much detail as possible in your message to help us assist you efficiently.

Support Hours

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM CST (U.S. Central Time), excluding holidays.

Scope of Support

Our support covers:

  • Technical issues or bugs in the app
  • Navigation or usage questions
  • Feedback and content suggestions

We do not provide clinical advice or mental health services. For urgent or medical mental health concerns, please consult a licensed professional or contact emergency services in your area.

Privacy & Data

We do not track or store any personal information entered by users. All data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Updates

This support policy may be updated periodically. Please check this page for the latest version.
