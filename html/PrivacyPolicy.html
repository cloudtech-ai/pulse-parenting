Privacy Policy for Pulse Parenting

Effective Date: 6/4/2025

Thank you for using Pulse Parenting. Our mission is to support parents in raising emotionally healthy children through expert-backed content and tools.

🔒 Use of Email and Device ID

We use your email address as your login name. We will only send you emails related to your account and important updates to the app. We also use you device ID to avoid the abusing of your account. In case you no longer need this app, you do have an option to delete your account. We will remove your email address and device id from our system.

🔒 No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or track any personal information from users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Name, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage behavior or analytics
  • Information entered into the app

📱 Local-Only Data Handling

All information you enter into the app (such as API Kyes, notes, preferences, or custom content) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

🛡️ Your Control

Because we do not collect or transmit your data, you have full control over your information on your own device. Deleting the app will remove all stored data.
